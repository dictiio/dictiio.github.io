    Instructions

    This game is an improved version of the legendary Crossy Road.

    The improvements include the addition of four unique power-ups, collectible coins, and purchasable skins. These skins are permanently kept upon purchase.

    Additionally, if you crave a greater challenge, you can switch to hard mode. In this mode, you must be quick—you won't have time to think! As the game goes on, cars and logs become faster!

    Your goal is to avoid obstacles and cross the road! Use the arrow keys or WASD to move around the map.
