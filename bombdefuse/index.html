Your goal is to defuse the bomb before you run out of time. To do so, you will have to guess a randomly chosen number between 1 and 100.

Based on the chosen difficulty level, you'll be allocated a specific timeframe to complete the task. Additionnally, every incorrect answer will deduct a few seconds from the timer.

After each incorrect guess, a helpful hint will be provided, indicating whether the target number is higher or lower than your current guess.

Good luck!
